About ISOP+

ISOP+ is a platform built to make learning, career growth, and inclusive opportunities more accessible for disabled youth and women. We believe potential has no boundaries — access should not either.

Our mission

To connect learners with opportunities, mentors, and supportive communities — removing the traditional barriers to access and success that disproportionately affect disabled youth and women.

📖

Our story

ISOP+ started as a university project with a simple question: why do so many capable people never get a fair shot? That question led us to build a platform that actively bridges the gap between ability and opportunity.

🚀

Our vision

A world where disability and gender are never barriers to professional growth. We envision a thriving, self-sustaining community of learners, mentors, and employers committed to true inclusion.
